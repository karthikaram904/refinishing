Billing FAQs

We collect a 30% advance once the quote is approved and work begins. The remaining 70% is due on completion, before delivery.
We accept UPI, NEFT/IMPS, all major credit/debit cards, and cash for in-studio pickups. Invoices are available for corporate clients.
The quoted price is fixed unless additional structural issues are discovered once we begin. We'll always notify you and get approval before any additional work that affects cost.
Our minimum job value is ₹500. There is no minimum for the number of pieces — we're happy to work on a single chair or a full home's worth of furniture.
