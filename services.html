Common Questions

Service FAQs

Turnaround depends on the service. Colour transformations typically take 5–7 working days. Full refinishing with structural work takes 7–12 days. Premium restorations are prioritised at 5–7 days.
Free pickup and delivery is included in our Premium package for addresses within Chennai city limits. For other services, we offer affordable pickup at ₹299 within Chennai and ₹699 for nearby districts.
We work with all solid wood, plywood, MDF, and veneer furniture — chairs, tables, cabinets, dressers, wardrobes, sofas, and more. We don't refinish plastic or metal furniture (though we can work with metal hardware).
Yes — always. Every project includes at least one digital before-after mockup for your approval before any work begins. The Studio and Premium packages include multiple options to choose from.
All our finishes carry a 6-month warranty against chipping, peeling, or adhesion failure under normal use. Premium restorations include a 12-month warranty. Structural repairs carry a 24-month warranty.
