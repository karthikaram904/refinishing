How It Began

Born from a Love of Old Things

ReGrain Studio began when founder Arjun Nair rescued a battered 1940s teak sideboard from a roadside pile in Mylapore. What started as a personal weekend project grew into an obsession — and then a calling.

Today, our 8-person team operates from a 3,000 sq ft studio in T.Nagar, Chennai. Every piece that comes through our doors is treated as a unique artefact with its own character, history, and potential. We believe that sustainable furniture begins with not throwing things away.

What Drives Us

Our Core Values

Sustainability

Every piece we restore is one less item in a landfill. We use low-VOC, eco-certified paints and finishes.

Craftsmanship

No shortcuts. Every surface is hand-sanded, every coat is inspected. Quality is non-negotiable in our studio.

Transparency

Our client dashboard lets you see every step of your project. No surprises — just clear, honest communication.

Care

Furniture holds memories. We treat every piece with the same respect we'd give a family honest heirloom.
